Our Story

Built on Passion,
Driven by Purpose

Creativa was born from a simple belief — that every brand deserves visual storytelling that truly resonates. Founded by Muhammad Afthab K, a passionate young creative from Kerala, the agency started as a one-person mission to bring world-class design and editing to creators and businesses of all sizes.

What began as a personal obsession with pixels, frames, and storytelling has grown into a full creative studio. Every project we take on is treated as a creative challenge — we don't just deliver files, we craft experiences that captivate audiences and convert viewers into fans.

We work with YouTubers, Instagram creators, startups, and small businesses who understand that quality visuals aren't an expense — they're an investment.
